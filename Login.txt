IMAP YAHOO Login (no need to type in your actual password)

======CREATE AN APP PASSWORD FOR YAHOO ACCOUNT=======

1. Go yo Yahoo - Login Page and enable 2FA(choose via phone number or app)
2. In "Security" menu look for "Manage app passwords" or "App password"
3. Click on "Generate app password"
4. Choose "Other App" or "Custom Name" and enter a name for your project
5. Yahoo will generate a secure app password for you.
6. Copy this password. This is the password you will use in the project.
